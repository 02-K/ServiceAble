How it all began

In 2021, four students from France, Colombia, Egypt and Italy had a common goal: to find a part-time job related to their field of expertise. They quickly realized that finding a job online was complicated. Their solution? They created their own online job board. Employers posted jobs and students could easily apply for them, in addition to being a job related to their studies.

ServiceAble International

Our way of working is simple. Our ambition is to connect employers with the best candidates who are looking for a job related to their studies. Are you looking for a job in Sweden or Spain? We can also help you. We are present all over Europe and we hope soon all over the world

Offering the right jobs to candidates means understanding their needs. Generation Z is now the largest and many of them are looking for work. We need to know what they want and what is right for them. Ambitious, fun-loving and security-seeking, Generation Z is always looking for new challenges.
